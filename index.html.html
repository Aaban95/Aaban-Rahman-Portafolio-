About Me

I'm an A-Level student with a deep interest in engineering — particularly aerodynamics, mechanical systems, and motorsport. My work is driven by curiosity and a commitment to building things that actually function.

Each project in this portfolio represents real engineering practice: defining a problem, designing a solution, building it, testing it, and improving it. I document iterations carefully because the process matters as much as the result.

My goal is to study Mechanical or Aeronautical Engineering at university, with a long-term focus on motorsport — contributing to race car development or propulsion systems.
